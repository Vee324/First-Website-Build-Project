Spicy

Medium

Traditional

    Indulge in the timeless delight of traditional Belgian fries, perfectly golden and crispy on the outside, soft and fluffy on the inside. Served piping hot, these fries are accompanied by a tantalizing array of sauces and dressings, ranging from classic mayonnaise to zesty tomato ketchup, tangy tartar sauce, and creamy aioli.
